This BMI Calculator website was developed by Onike Fareed, a 20yr old physiology student & developer from Lagos,Nigeria and Miracle Ajanaku,also a physiology student, from Ekiti,Nigeria and a HEAL representative as part of their project presentation for the upcoming HEAL conference. It aims to promote awareness of healthy body weight through easy access to BMI evaluation. The design emphasizes simplicity, accuracy, and educational value for users worldwide.
