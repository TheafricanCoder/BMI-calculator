BMI Categories

This table helps interpret your BMI score and its health implication:

BMI Range Classification Meaning
Below 18.5UnderweightMay indicate malnutrition or medical concerns
18.5 – 24.9Normal weightGenerally healthy, lowest health risk
25.0 – 29.9OverweightPotential increased risk of health issues
30.0 – 34.9Obesity (Class I)Higher risk of chronic diseases
35.0 – 39.9Obesity (Class II)Significant health concerns
40.0 and aboveObesity (Class III)Severe health risks, medical guidance needed
